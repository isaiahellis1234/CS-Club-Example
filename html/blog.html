CS Club US Davis Tour

The Computer Science Club of Solano Community College was founded on August 12th, 2024, by our President, Jennifer Petsko.

Since then, our club has grown tremendously, amassing over 100 Discord members, surpassing 50 official members, and regularly hosting well-attended meetings.

Recently, the Computer Science Club, joined by the MESA Club and Phi Theta Kappa, traveled to UC Davis for an exciting tour.
